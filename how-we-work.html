How We Work

Our Process

STEP 1

What Do you Need?

We'll consult with your over the phone to see if Awkward media is for you. We'll try to achieve your vision for the video as close as we can for the lowest rate. We'll provide you with a story board or examples of our pre-existing work similar to what we think we can achieve.

STEP 2

We Shoot!

We travel to a location to shoot your video (we'll travel anywhere in Bristol for free, travel expenses may have to be covered outside of Bristol). We shoot your video with half the agreed rate payed up front first.
NOTE: We are not sound recordists. To achieve a clean mix for your music video you will either need a live sound recordist or aclean existing mix of your chosen song.

STEPÂ 3

We Edit and Revise

After we've shot the video we'll start editing right away. before we edit we'll give you a story board or examples of what you video may look like in comparison to other material. Due to having limited time in the edit we charge for revisions (What we believe to be altercations with quick turn arounds are free).

STEP 4

Your Video

We send you your video in 4K, edited and colour graded. Once we have been past step 4 the edit is locked and we cannot make any further adjustments.
